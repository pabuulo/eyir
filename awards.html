The top exclusive luxury consultancy since 2001.

For over two decades, Eyir has remained a closely guarded secret among the world's elite travelers and connoisseurs of luxury. As a private and exclusive consultancy, we have discreetly collaborated with top-tier hotels, restaurants, and cruise lines, setting the gold standard for opulence and refinement. However, the winds of change are upon us, and in 2024, Eyir is taking an extraordinary leap forward by going public.

Since our inception, Eyir has been synonymous with exclusivity, catering to a select clientele whose expectations are nothing short of extraordinary. For more than 20 years, we've been the silent architects behind unforgettable travel experiences, dining adventures, and cruise voyages. Our discretion and commitment to excellence have earned us the trust of discerning travelers and the respect of industry insiders. Behind closed doors, we've tirelessly curated a world of luxury, shaping unforgettable memories and elevating expectations. As we enter the year 2024, Eyir is embracing a transformational journey that will redefine the luxury landscape. We are proud to announce our decision to go public, marking a significant shift in our approach. Our mission is clear: to rate, evaluate, and award the very best luxury services worldwide.

Setting a New Standard for Excellence

In our new public incarnation, Eyir will bring transparency and objectivity to the world of luxury. Our expert evaluators will meticulously assess hotels, restaurants, and cruise lines, setting rigorous standards and criteria. We will provide a trusted, unbiased guide for travelers seeking the pinnacle of luxury experiences.

From the luxurious hotels of Europe to the culinary delights of Asia, and the cruise voyages traversing the world's oceans, Eyir's evaluative expertise will reach every corner of the globe.
