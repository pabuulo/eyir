Welcome to Lionstower, the heir of literature.

Welcome to Eyir.

Eyir by Lionstower is an exclusive private non-fiction publisher, dedicated to catering to a distinguished library and cultivating exceptional thoughts.

Liontower is not just a publisher; we are a catalyst for the mind, a beacon for provocative thought, and a sanctuary for the art of poetry. With a journey spanning five remarkable years, we have dedicated ourselves to curating and championing nonfiction that challenges, inspires, and educates.

At Liontower, we are fueled by the relentless pursuit of new ideas, the unquenchable thirst for innovation, and the undying passion for transformative thought. Our mission is to ignite the spark of ingenuity within every reader and bring to the world an innovative mindset that transcends boundaries and sparks meaningful change.

Our commitment to the written word is unwavering, and our dedication to authors and their powerful narratives is our driving force. We believe in the power of words to shape the world, and we are proud to be a platform for voices that are bold, diverse, and unafraid to challenge the status quo.

Liontower is not just a publisher; we are a sanctuary for the seekers of wisdom, a home for the pioneers of thought, and a champion of those who dare to be different. Join us in our journey to inspire, provoke, and transform through the art of nonfiction and poetry. Welcome to the Liontower family, where innovation meets literature, and ideas become a force for change.
